pldrGallery

pldrGallery is web application to browse files focused on viewing images and videos on custom private servers. Originaly it started just as simple PHP script but as time was flowing, I was searching for more robust solution and pldrGallery was born!

Basic goal is to not give gigabytes of your personal media files from holidays to public servers, but keep them on your own computer with ability to share them to anyone out in the Internet.

All tested galeries were creating thumbnails and indexes, didn't care about security or simply didnt't work with so much collected data.
When I started this project, my family gallery was about 500 GB big with ~80 000 files and ~1200 folders and I knew it will be counting.

Features

  • Fast browsing folders and files on your harddisc without any need to reload page
  • Image, video and audio is fully supported thanks to HML5. Other files can be downloaded directly to computer
  • Support for Google login or just passwords
  • Even if you know URL to file or folder, you will not access it unless you have permission.
  • Allow everything or just some folders depends on logged users or passwords
  • Data saver to run even on slow internet connection (server upload or client download)
  • Relax with presentation mode
  • Show images on map based on EXIF data
  • Save your eyes with dark theme
  • Use keyboard for almost everything
  • And much more...
